I was born and raised in Warendorf, a cute little town somewhere in Germany. It's a great place to live if you're into horseback riding, but not so much if you're interested in physics. So I moved to Munich at the age of 18, and did some cool physics there. Four years later, I got bored of Munich and moved to Cambridge, Massachusetts, which is also good for physics.

In my free time, I like to write code, try to play the piano, and hang out with friends. I also like snowboarding, squirrels, the number tau, and free food. I don't like pi.

Born to be a pirate, grew up to be a theoretical physicist.
